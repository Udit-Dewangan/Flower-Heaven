About Us

Welcome to Flower Haven! We're dedicated to showcasing rare and beautiful flowers for enthusiasts and collectors alike. Explore our collection and learn about their unique characteristics and care.

Corpse Flower (Amorphophallus titanum)

Known for its large size and foul odor, the corpse flower is native to Sumatra.

Middlemist Red (Middlemist camellia)

This rare flower is only known to exist in two locations worldwide.

Iron Cross (Oxalis deppei)

This rare flower is only known to exist in two locations worldwide.

Blue Vanda Orchid (Vanda coerulea)

A striking blue orchid, renowned for its unique color and rarity.

Juliet Rose

Created by David Austin, this rose took over 15 years to breed and is highly sought after.

Rothschild's Orchid (Paphiopedilum rothschildianum)

This endangered orchid is one of the rarest in the world, found only in Borneo.
